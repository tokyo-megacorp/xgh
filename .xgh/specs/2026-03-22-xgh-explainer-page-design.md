# xgh Explainer Page

> Interactive scroll-driven landing page that tells the xgh story below the TUI hero section.

## Status: Draft — Needs Brainstorming

## Direction

The explainer section should use the **scroll-driven storytelling** genre (Apple product pages, Stripe docs, Linear landing) to progressively reveal xgh's value prop. Each scroll step reveals a concept with animation.

**Inspiration sources** (genre, not copy):
- Apple product keynote pages — progressive disclosure, large type, cinematic reveals
- Stripe documentation — clean developer-focused aesthetics
- Linear landing page — motion design, dark mode, precision
- losslesscontext.ai — scroll-triggered concept explanation (admired execution, must differentiate)

## Constraints

- Must have its own visual identity — xgh's brand, not a derivative
- TUI demo is the hero section (above the fold)
- Explainer lives below, unfolds as you scroll
- Should feel like discovering, not reading

## Open Questions (for brainstorming session)

- What's the visual identity? Color palette, typography, motion language?
- What are the 4-6 concept beats to reveal? (memory, dispatch, routing, learning loop, etc.)
- Static site or framework? (Astro, Next.js, vanilla scroll observers?)
- Dark mode only or adaptive?
