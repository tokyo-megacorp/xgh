What xgh does

YOUR AI
RIDES
FASTER.

⟳
Persistent Memory

xgh stores every session in a hybrid episodic + semantic memory layer. Your AI remembers what you did last week — and why.

⚡
Context Compression

context-mode routes all large outputs through a sandbox. Stop wasting tokens on raw Bash output. 60–90% savings on dev operations.

⟁
Multi-Agent Dispatch

Delegate to Codex, Gemini, or OpenCode as parallel agents. Worktree isolation, flag detection, retry logic — all handled.

◈
9 Specialized Agents

code-reviewer, investigation-lead, pr-reviewer, pipeline-doctor, codex-driver — purpose-built agents that know your repo.

◎
Automated Retrieval

Scheduled ingestion from Slack, Jira, GitHub, Confluence, Figma. What's happening on your team lands in context automatically.

⟡
Superpowers Workflow

Built-in methodology: brainstorm → plan → subagent execution → two-stage review. Ship features the right way, every time.
